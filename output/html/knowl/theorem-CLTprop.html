Theorem 1.1.4. The Central Limit Theorem (Proportion).

For a sufficiently large sample (typically \(pn, p(1-n)\geq 10\) or at least 10 “successes” and “failures”) the sample distribution of\(\hat{p}\) is approximately normally distributed with pararmeters

\begin{equation*} \mu_{\hat{p}}=p,\ \ \ SE_{\hat{p}}=\sqrt{\frac{p(1-p)}{n}}. \end{equation*}
