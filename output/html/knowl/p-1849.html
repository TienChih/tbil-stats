For either \(\beta_0, \beta_1\text{,}\) we compute the C% confidence interval for that parameter as follows:

\begin{equation*} [\text{point estimate}_i - SE_it^*, \text{point estimate}_i + SE_it^*] \end{equation*}

where \(t^*\) is the \(t\)-value such that \(P(-t^*\lt t\lt t^*)=C\%\) for the standard \(t\)-variable with the appropriate degrees of freedom.
